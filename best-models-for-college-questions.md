## üí° Tips for Getting Better Explanations

### 1. **Be Specific in Your Prompts**
‚ùå Bad: "Explain photosynthesis"
‚úÖ Good: "Explain the light-dependent reactions of photosynthesis, focusing on the electron transport chain"

### 2. **Request Step-by-Step Breakdowns**
```
"Please explain this calculus problem step-by-step, showing all work and explaining the reasoning behind each step."
```

### 3. **Ask for Multiple Approaches**
```
"Show me 3 different ways to solve this problem, and explain when each method is most useful."
```

### 4. **Request Analogies**
```
"Explain quantum entanglement using everyday analogies that a college freshman would understand."
```

### 5. **Ask for Verification**
```
"Here's my solution to this problem. Can you check my work and identify any errors?"
```

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Considerations

### **Academic Integrity:**
- Use AI as a **learning tool**, not a replacement for learning
- Always cite AI assistance when required by your institution
- Verify information from multiple sources
- Understand the concepts, don't just copy answers

### **Limitations:**
- AI can make mistakes (hallucinations)
- May not have access to latest information
- Cannot replace human expertise in specialized fields
- Should be verified for critical assignments

### **Best Practices:**
1. Cross-reference answers with textbooks
2. Ask your professor if AI use is permitted
3. Use AI to understand concepts, not just get answers
4. Practice problems yourself after understanding
